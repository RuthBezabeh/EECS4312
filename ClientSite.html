Project Name: SmartStock

Project Description: Retail businesses, regardless of their size, face the ongoing challenge of effectively managing their inventory.The traditional manual inventory management processes are time-consuming, error-prone, and often result in overstocking or understocking of products. This inefficiency can lead to increased operational costs, lost sales opportunities, and dissatisfied customers. To address these issues, there is a pressing need for a smart retail inventory management system that leverages technology to automate inventory tracking, predict demand, and optimize stock levels.
